The Past

My first introduction to Web Development was several years ago, back in high-school when I took a short course "How to make your own webpage". The Web Development at that time was different, to say the least. We used HTML tables to create a layout, and merged cells creatively to make a design. I really enjoyed it, however, I did not believe it will ever catch up, and that anyone would use the Internet for anything useful. I admit it, I was wrong!

The Present

In 2019, I was finally able to go back to college to study Web Development. I am very proud that with the help of my instructors I was able to learn programming languages, database and server-side technologies, testing techniques and elements of web design. I have no doubts now that web development is what I genuinely like, and passionate about.

The Future

I am thrilled to see what the future technologies are going to bring to the Web Development field. With something new coming almost every day, this field remains dynamic and exciting. I like to stay in touch with new developments by communicating with other developers by attending coding events and interest groups.
